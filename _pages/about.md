I am a first-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where I am a member of the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.hkust.edu.hk/) advised by [Prof. Junxian He](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~jhexian/). I received my B.Eng. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2024.

Research Interests
======
My research interests lie in natural language processing and machine learning, with a particular focus on:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning** — developing methods that improve the reasoning capabilities of large language models, including synthetic data generation and verifiable reasoning tasks.
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLMs)** — understanding and mitigating hallucination in vision-language models, particularly in chart understanding and visual reasoning tasks.
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability** — probing the inner representations of language models to detect and improve the truthfulness of their outputs.
