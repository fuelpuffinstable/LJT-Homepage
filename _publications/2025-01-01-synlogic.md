SynLogic is a framework for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale, designed to support training and evaluation of logical reasoning capabilities in large language models. The work introduces a data generation pipeline that produces reasoning traces with verifiable correctness signals, enabling scalable supervision for logical reasoning and other reasoning-intensive tasks.
